Surah Al-Kahf (The Cave)

Part Sixteen | Page 302

قال هذا فراق بيني وبينك سأنبئك بتأويل ما لم تستطع عليه صبرا

٧٨

[Al-Khidr] said, “This is the parting between me and you. I will inform you of the interpretation of that which you could not bear patiently.”

English: Sahih International

أما السفينة فكانت لمساكين يعملون في البحر فأردت أن أعيبها وكان وراءهم ملك يأخذ كل سفينة غصبا

٧٩

“As for the ship, it belonged to poor people working at sea, and I intended to damage it because there was a king behind them who seized every [sound] ship by force.”

English: Sahih International
